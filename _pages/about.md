I am a third-year Statistics PhD student at Stanford University advised by Professor [Art Owen](https://artowen.su.domains). 

My research focuses on Monte Carlo methods, especially randomized quasi–Monte Carlo (RQMC) techniques, with applications to high-dimensional numerical integration and PDE estimation.

Before starting my PhD, I graduated from École Polytechnique, where I completed the engineering program (Cycle Ingénieur) with a focus on Applied Mathematics and Statistics.
